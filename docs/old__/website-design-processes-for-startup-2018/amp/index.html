10 Steps Website Design Processes for Startup-2018

The website is crucial for every business because marketing in business is an essential thing while doing marketing every one should move according to the latest and influenced marketing. Nowadays every one knew digital marketing better than traditional marketing because of internet penetration across global besides having lots of advantages of digital marketing. The website is one of the biggest tools of the digital marketing process. web design is a little tough task. You are constantly developing your design until you reach the viewpoint. The web design’s progress depends on how well you connect elements such as lines, fonts, colors, images, design, and textures to describe a company’s mission and vision. The attention is extremely essential during each part of the process of website design. You want to get into website design for your startup literally you have many doubts Where do I start? How do I start? How can I choose the best web design company? Here I explain the details of the 10 steps website design process for startups-2018.

Your startup needs a website which includes professional and eye-catchy and this can be a tricky perspective for your website design to complete. Luckily there are 10 steps website design process you can follow on which can assist the website design process move easily. You have taken the strong step to commence your own startups business, fine now let’s take a little effort to design the website for your startups.

Keep Design Simple

The most common suggestion from my client’s side about their website design is ‘makes it simple and unique’. The website shows the brand name of your startup so it must impress the guests. A simple website without unnecessary distractions is less overwhelming, particularly for first-time guests. By a less intricate design, users will be able to keep their focus on the content they are looking for. Presently be sure to make the information on your site clear and short to engage their attention; because a really effective website design doesn’t just look great, it also helps the content efficiently so make simple your websites.

Content Creation

Content is always the king of your website can seldom be the stimulating part of your website. It requires to attract the visitors and convey the right kind of information at the top level so guests actually read it instead of jumping on to the other website. When you write content, imagine twice about it from an advertising viewpoint. Besides to content, you also need to add attractive images and graphics are commonly used to increase the text content or seldom even replace it. Earning the pages professional viewing is especially significant as visual influence and attraction is a key part of any website. The website is an area where you should use content, images, videos, graphics, infographics and unless you are very good at generating these things, you might need to hunt a web design company to assist you.

Page Load Time

When you design a website page loading speed is crucial because guests have no patience for your websites with lower load speeds performance.A study said, touching half of the web users anticipate the website to load in 2 seconds or less. If it isn’t loaded within 3 seconds, users lead to abandoning the respective website. That indicates you’re not only losing your current guests and reducing conversion rates, though you run the risk of your website losing traffic from those customers who may have referred your website to others. With that in mind, let’s try to get the speedy website for your startup and optimize your website for the best potential performance.

Make User-Friendly

User-friendly website developing is the art of making your website and easy to use. Knowing your customer’s online presence provides you insight into what does and what doesn’t. The mystery lies in proper preparation and thorough experiment behind the user-friendly website. Largest established companies have a step in their project progress especially the website designed to identify and eliminate possible user-friendly issues. It typically has no website design elements or color; preferably it concentrates on something. The sole focus is on content, structure, flow, and functionality without other visuals to distract the eye. so try to design the user-friendly website for your startup.

Clear Call to Action

The purpose of designing an e-commerce website or any website is to obtain it helpful for users to buy products and services or other purposes. A simplistic approach of encouraging progress is by including Clear Call to Action(CTA) in the website such as buttons, images, and links followed by an actionable text that demonstrates to the guests the outcome of their actions. If your website has been receiving a lot of traffic, but the conversion rate does not resemble to be good enough, among the many possible reasons, there often is the absence of well-designed and strategically located CTAs. Placing of CTAs is very important for your website so the consultation of the website designer keep it proper.

Case Studies

Case studies are a marketing hook of your startup. Companies use them to show how their product or service has been completed successfully and delivered to customers. Rather than just talking about a product or services, case studies empower businesses to show how their product or services can be used. An efficient case study will incorporate similar elements of your startup while designing website placed correct place or arrange separate page for the case study in your website.

Links to Social Pages

Social media profiles work properly with your startup website to improve your online business of startup. Applying links on your website plays a vital role in its search engine rankings. Google will automatically connect your social media pages with your company when recognizing the links on your website. There is the huge number of ways to combine your social media presence on your startup website. Have many advantages to attaching social media such as Facebook, Google+, LinkedIn, Instagram, Pinterest, Twitter and etc to your website. The help of a website builder to add social media pages to your website its an easy process.

Make the Website Responsive

Today most of the people spend the time on browsing your favorite websites or social media or ordering things through the mobile phone. Most of the people owned the smartphone, it is important that your website looks good and works well on all devices. That is why all websites must fit responsive web design. Responsive web design is the practice of building a website suitable to run on every device and every screen size, no matter how large or small, mobile or desktop. Responsive web design is concentrated around providing an intuitive and satisfying user experience for everyone regardless of the device they use to access your website so must design the responsive website for your startup.

Add Some Fun

The main goal of all projects that we need them to make an impression on people. We want the websites that we create to be remarkable. A fun activity is usually an enjoyable one and an enjoyable experience is usually an interesting thing. Hence, it attains to mind that one of the ways to create a memorable experience is to make it a fun experience. we take a look at how adding a little fun into the mix can help us produce more engaging and expectedly more successful so add some fun to your website.

SEO Friendly

Most of the people know how much important of Search Engine Optimization(SEO) when comes on online presence. Although there is no getting ranking without satisfying the Google bot. This means is developing an SEO plan that satisfies the requirements of your brand and consumers. the step is creating an SEO compliance website. If the website you have isn’t optimized, then it’ll fight to get to the top of the search engine page results. Hold in your mind that users seldom go beyond the first page of search engines. So get it as SEO friendly websites.
